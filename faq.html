Frequently Asked Questions

What is My Account?

My Account is a one stop for all the information related to your order(s). It gives you the complete control to do the following:

Edit your personal information like Name, address, etc.
Change password
View your Trendin Credits
Initiate a return / refund of your order
Cancel an order

Is it necessary to have an account to shop on Shift Your Shopping?

Yes. One needs to register shop on Shift Your Shopping.

How do I know my order has been confirmed?

Once your payment is authorized and your order is completed, you will receive an email from Shift your shopping confirming the order placement. You would be provided with the order number, details of the order and the amount you have paid. You will also receive a sms confirming the same.

Do you take orders over the phone?

No. We do not take orders the phone. Customer would have to place the order by themselves. One could call the customer care support for any difficulties faced during placing the order.

What is the estimated delivery time?

The delivery time varies from cities to cities. Typically it take 2 , 3 days for all metros & Tier 1 cities. Tier 2 & 3 cities may take 3 to 5 days. Some cities in north east India & J&K etc may take 5 to 7 days. We also ship through Speed Post to few pin codes where no other courier partner can deliver. For any shipment which is getting shipped through India Post, you will be notified at the time of providing your address. India post shipments may take 7 to 12 business days for delivery.
You could enter your pin code at the product view page to know the exact estimate time of delivery.

What are the shipping charges?

Shift your shopping.com offers free shipping within India on all products purchased on its website.
